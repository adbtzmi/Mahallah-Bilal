Frequently Ask Question

What is Mahallah?
Mahallah refers to the residential estates of the students in IIUM. There are currently 16 Mahallahs in IIUM to accommodate all full-time undergraduate students. Click here for the list of Mahallahs in IIUM.
Are there Wifi coverage in Mahallah?
The IIUM campus provides Wifi service for all the students including in Mahallah, although different rooms are subjected to different network strengths depending on the position of the router.
What types of rooms are available for students?
  • Quad room for Undergraduate students, which features 4 compartments for 4 students.
  • Twin Sharing (without partition) for 2 undergraduate students.
  • Single room for Postgraduate students.
Is cooking allowed in Mahallah?
Cooking is not allowed in your rooms (cook at your own risk), although all mahallah have their own canteens which sells affordable foods for the students.
What is LIDV?
LIDV is the term for Live In During Vacation
